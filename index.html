FAQs

Where does this fit in my daily routine?+

Use immediately after cleansing, before serum and moisturizer — morning and night. It preps skin and locks in hydration without heaviness.

Which skin types is it suitable for?+

Designed for all skin types — including sensitive, reactive, and acne-prone. Non-comedogenic and tested on diverse skin needs.

Has it undergone testing?+

Yes. Dermatologist-tested with independent consumer studies. 4.6★ average from 120 verified reviews.

Is it vegan and fragrance-free?+

Yes. Vegan, fragrance-free, and formulated without sulfates, parabens, or mineral oil. Light natural mugwort note only.

What is Artemisia-Cica™ and how does it work?+

A proprietary complex combining Ganghwa mugwort water and centella asiatica. Mugwort delivers instant soothing signals; centella supports barrier recovery over time — measured, not diluted across dozens of actives.
